About Us

JEY GROUPS is a leading organization successfully operating 18+ major projects across multiple industries. We are now launching our new E-commerce initiative, GRABBASKETS, with a massive rollout across Tamil Nadu simultaneously connecting 3000 employees, 40,000 shops, and 4 million products.

As part of this expansion, we are offering students an excellent opportunity to join our workforce through structured training programs followed by assured placements. The available job roles are listed below:

What We Do

WEBSITE HANDLING (APPLICATION & SERVER OPERATIONS)

  • You will be trained to manage websites, applications, and server operations.
  • Work involves monitoring, updating, and ensuring smooth functioning of platforms.
  • The job operates in rotational shifts (Morning & Noon).
  • You will gain complete knowledge of how an application works from scratch.

DIGITAL MARKETING WITH AI

  • No coding knowledge required.
  • You will be trained in AI-powered digital marketing tools and strategies.
  • The role focuses on creativity, content planning, market research, and campaign execution.
  • Ideal for students who want a creative and fast-growing career path.

DEVELOPER (WEB, APPLICATION & SERVER DEVELOPMENT)

  • You will learn to build complete systems including websites, applications, and backend servers.
  • Training includes hands-on development with industry-standard tools and technologies.
  • Suitable for students passionate about coding and technology.

Career Opportunities

Website Handling

Responsible for managing, updating, and maintaining company websites to ensure smooth performance and a consistent user experience. Handles content updates, UI improvements, and basic technical issues across all devices.

Responsibilities:
  • Manage and update website content regularly
  • Ensure responsive and cross-browser compatibility
  • Monitor website performance and fix issues
  • Coordinate with design and marketing teams
  • Maintain website security and backups

Digital Marketing (AI)

We are seeking a Digital Marketing Executive with hands-on experience in AI-powered marketing tools. The role focuses on driving online growth through data-driven strategies, automation, and performance marketing using modern AI tools and platforms.

Responsibilities:
  • Plan and execute digital marketing campaigns
  • Use AI tools for content creation, ads, and analytics
  • Manage social media, SEO, and paid campaigns
  • Track performance and optimize campaigns using insights
  • Improve brand visibility and lead generation

Full Stack Developer

We are hiring a Full Stack Developer who can build and maintain scalable web applications. The ideal candidate should be comfortable working with both frontend and backend technologies and delivering clean, efficient, and secure code.

Responsibilities:
  • Develop frontend interfaces using modern frameworks
  • Build backend APIs and manage databases
  • Integrate third-party services and APIs
  • Optimize application performance and security
  • Collaborate with designers and project teams
